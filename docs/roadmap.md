The exam format

Every exam would be different each time you take it.

For example, the x86-64 exam might include:

10 conceptual questions
10 register questions
10 assembly reading questions
10 debugging questions
10 code-writing questions

A sample might look like:

What does the RSP register store?
Which instruction pushes a value onto the stack?
What happens when CALL executes?
Convert 11101101₂ to hexadecimal.
Why does a function usually save RBP?
